Game Plan

Mission Reds, named after the baseball team that played at Recreatation Field in the Mission district of San Francisco in the 1930s, is focused on building creating great companies that create, expand and disrupt markets.

Batting Cages

In each Batting Cage, the Mission Reds team will focus on finding product market fit for 2-3 target markets. Building on the principles of Customer Development and Lean Startup methodology, the team will focus on rapid development and deployment, active and deliberate customer feedback loops and minimum core value refinement in the Batting Cages

Team

The Mission Reds team is composed of passionate, diven product and tech capable entrepreneurs leading the customer development process in the Batting Cages. An advisory board of industry specialists will work with entrepreneurs in the Batting Cages and as newCos are set up with IP created in the Batting Cages.
